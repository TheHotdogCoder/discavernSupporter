About Discavern

What is DisCavern?

DisCavern is a Arcade Lounge made by G9 - Cavendish for the Student Business Fair 2024. The services offered here is good for students who want to have fun with their friends by doing activities such as gaming, making arts, and more!.

What we can see in DisCavern?

Arcade

Lokki's Arcade consists of many games that you can play. From video games like Tekken to arcade games like Mini-Basketball, you'll surely be satisfied to go for the win!

Lounge

Lucho's Lounge is a good place to get some rest with friends. And with many board games to play, you will never get bored going here!

Photobooth

Join Zaika as you get some pictures with your friends that will definitely be a memory you'll be keeping!

Bracelet Making And Painting

Gaming is not your type? You can also enjoy making bracelets and paint lovely images with Miyo!

Music

What about some music? Try listening Yoko's beats and songs!

TGIF Delivery

Now hungry from all that fun? Just wait and you'll get food and drinks from our friends from Thank God It's Faraday!
